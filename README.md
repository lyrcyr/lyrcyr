# Hi, I am Junhui Liu

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Control Science and Engineering at Northwestern Polytechnical University, China.

Before joining NPU, I received my B.Eng. degree in Aircraft Control and Information Engineering from the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Sichuan University, in 2023.

## About Me

My previous research mainly focused on control compliance and trajectory optimization in physical human–robot interaction. Most of my experimental studies were conducted on haptic devices, where human interaction forces and motion responses were used to investigate compliant and adaptive interaction strategies.

Currently, I am interested in applying deep learning methods to human–robot interaction. My future research will focus on compliant interaction, safety-aware interaction, and learning-based control methods for robotic systems interacting with humans.

## Research Interests

- Physical human–robot interaction
- Compliant control
- Human intention understanding
- Trajectory optimization
- Learning-based robot control
- Deep learning for human–robot interaction
